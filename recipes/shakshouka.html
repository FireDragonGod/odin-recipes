Spicy One-Pot Shakshouka

Shakshouka is a Maghrebi dish of eggs poached in a sauce of tomatoes, olive oil, peppers, onion and garlic, commonly spiced with cumin, paprika and cayenne pepper. See more on Wikipedia

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, halved and finely chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 red chile pepper, seeded and finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
